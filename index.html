Our Story

Owner and Operator Joseph Gilmore, started laying hard wood floors in 2000 as an apprentice, he would go on to travel to Georgia to work for Dave Thomas. In 2005 Joe moves back to Kalispell Montana to start Quality hardwood flooring. Delivering amazing designs, and restoring and finishing older floors. We are licensed and insured, and have also won several prestiage awards one being the 2018 Parade Of Home Builders award for the best craftsmanship.

Installation

Efficient processes with the absolute best quality for each install. Staple down or nail down installation, glue down and floating floor. We specialize in custom patterns, unquie features and complicated inlays. We ensure that all installations help your project stay on time. We specialize in customized wooden staircases, fit and finish.

Repair

The awesome stuff about wood, is the way we are able to repair, restore and refinish the floors. In order to asset the situation we will review the damage or restoration job and give the best possible outcome. Please call us or email us for the best possible estimates.

Sand & Finishing

Sand and finishing is what makes the process complete. We have selected superior finishes to ensure that, the wear and appeal of the floor suits the need of every customer. We use the top of line equipment for sanding.
