Unmatched Expertise

M C Tile was founded in 1993 by Sicilian immigrant, Antonio Montante, who moved to the United States from Trapani, Sicily in 1965 at the age of 22. Antonio worked tirelessly to develop skills he learned from his father and soon after mastered the art of the trade. Like his father, Antonio taught his sons, Gaspare and Carmelo, how to be masters of their craft. In 2013, with over 17 years of experience, it is with immense pride and profound appreciation that Gaspare earned the privilege of carrying on his father's business, work ethic and exceptional skills as new owner of M C Tile
